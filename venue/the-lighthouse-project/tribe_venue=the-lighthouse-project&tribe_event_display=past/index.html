January 16: Anti-Islamophobia Media Workshop (Raleigh)

January 16, 2018 @ 6:30 pm - 8:30 pm
The Lighthouse Project, 202 N Tarboro Rd, Raleigh, NC 27610-2354, United States
Raleigh, NC 27610-2354 United States + Google Map

Movement to End Racism and Islamophobia invites everyone to join this workshop to brainstorm, compose, and submit Letters to the Editor across North Carolina newspapers. Please also bring your ideas on how to use digital media to challenge Islamophobia, including videos, spoken word, etc. Our goal is to change the media narrative around anti-Muslim bigotry! We will encourage folks to compose letters putting forward a narrative that rejects anti-Muslim bigotry alongside anti-Semitism, racism, anti-queer and anti-trans hatred, and US wars…
