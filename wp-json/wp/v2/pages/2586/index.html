We\u2019ve heard a lot about refugees over the last couple years, but how often do you get to meet them?<\/span><\/p>\n

Meet Baruani, Merz, Satam, and Yaman in our new video that follows recent refugees volunteering with Habitat for Humanity! Merz, Satam and Yaman are all from Syria, and Baruani is from the Republic of Congo. With the help of Church World Service (CWS), they have all recently moved to Durham to start their new lives. CWS also coordinates their regular volunteer days with Habitat. You\u2019ll also meet Diana who originally from Burma as a refugee, but now works for CWS helping other refugees settle into their new communities.<\/span><\/p>\n
