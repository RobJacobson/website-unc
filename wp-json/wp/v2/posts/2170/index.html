On Saturday\u00a0Uniting NC brought together a group of recent immigrants and longtime residents for a day-long Habitat for Humanity build. Our team worked all over the house, from the attic to the crawl space, and got more done than we’d ever seen in a single day of Habitat. Impressed Habitat staff and volunteers from other groups loved our diversity and were eager to learn more about Uniting NC. Good work, fun times and new friends.
