Uniting NC has just learned about a very exciting opportunity, but we need your help to take advantage of it.<\/p>\n

Individual donations from people like you make our work possible. And we\u2019re thrilled to tell you that, if you give now, we can double your money. But there\u2019s a hitch \u2014 it\u2019s set up as a challenge that only you can help us win!<\/p>\n

Welcoming America<\/a> has offered to match up to $5,000 in donations to Uniting NC. But they\u2019re making the same offer to organizations in 13 states, and they don\u2019t have enough funds for all the organizations. So please consider contributing this week, so that we can be sure to double the impact of your contribution.<\/p>\n

For accounting reasons, Welcoming America can only match donations of at least $200. But even if you can\u2019t make a large enough contribution for matching, please consider making a smaller donation. Every dollar really does count for a small non-profit like Uniting NC.<\/p>\n

What could these contributions mean for North Carolina? It could mean a dozen more events in 2011 in which we bring together recent immigrants and long-term residents to learn from one another. It could mean getting our message out to thousands more North Carolinians via billboards, the radio and maybe even television. It would mean concrete steps toward building the type of communities in which we can all proudly call North Carolina home.<\/p>\n
