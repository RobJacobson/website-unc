<\/a><\/p>\n

The one-month course is designed to take students from no experience to coding their first app. In addition, students have the chance to learn from mentors and meet entrepreneurs who have started their own businesses. The class is intended as a first step toward a career in tech or to provide students with the tech skills that will make them stronger applicants for jobs or studies in other fields.<\/p>\n

Who can apply?<\/strong>\u00a0Code the Dream is open to young people 15 to 25-ish. We provide a gateway to the tech sector for people who might not otherwise have the opportunity, particularly minority and immigrant youth.
