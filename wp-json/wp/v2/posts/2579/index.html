In 2013 a family in North Carolina faced a monumental decision when the father\/husband Miguel Cortes was asked to leave the country in 120 days after driving without a license. The \u00a0documentary 120 Days follows their decision whether to stay in North Carolina or return to Mexico — providing unique insight into the difficult decisions facing many\u00a0undocumented immigrant families living in the United States.<\/p>\n
