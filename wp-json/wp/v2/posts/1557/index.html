On Saturday, as part of National Welcoming Week<\/a>, Uniting NC pulled together North Carolinians from all around the world to help Inter-Faith Food Shuttle<\/a> gather fresh produce for needy families in our area. Together with an amazing group of volunteers with\u00a0Change the Triangle<\/a>, we had volunteers from Hong Kong, Mexico, Japan, the Dominican Republic, Nepal and Bhutan!<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

And guess what? We helped fill an entire truck with 12,650 pounds of sweet potatoes — which Inter-Faith Food Shuttle reports is the most that’s ever been gleaned at any single event in the history of the program!<\/em><\/p>\n

Thanks to everyone who came out! It was a fun and amazingly productive way to spend a Saturday morning — getting to meet neighbors from around the world all while helping provide fresh, healthy food for needy members of our community.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

A special thanks also to Locopops <\/a>for providing a nice treat for our hard-working volunteers. A Locopops break was just what we needed to finish off the job.<\/p>\n

We really appreciate it. They were delicious!<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n
