The Women of Color Empowerment Brunch provides a safe space for women who identify with the African diaspora to fellowship over food and positive energy while discussing issues that affect their lives and well-being as Black women and women of color. The intention is to provide a space for conversation, education, and leadership, as well as empowerment.<\/p>\n

The brunch will feature Durham County Commissioner Brenda Howerton, North Carolina Supreme Court Justice Anita Earls, and former Black Panther Chair & CEO of Oakland & the World Enterprises Elaine Brown. The mistress of ceremonies will be Omisade Burney-Scott and a host of others<\/p>\n

Enjoy food, mimosas, cash bar, music, great conversation, speaker, and a spoken word performance<\/p>\n
