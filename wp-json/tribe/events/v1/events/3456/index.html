The Social Action Committee is excited to invite the Beth Meyer Community to a multicultural Shabbat experience. The committee’s mission is to pursue righteousness through action to make the world a better place for all of its inhabitants. We live in a vibrant, multicultural country and sharing differences can help deepen our level of understanding within our diverse community. The dinner’s theme “We Are All Immigrants” sets the stage for members of our congregation to share a dish from their family’s cultural or ethnic heritage.<\/p>\n
