In the spirit of welcome and celebration, the City of Durham created Durham Refugee Day in 2017 to celebrate the contributions of refugees and immigrants to our community and publicly stand against anti-immigration policies that impact these members of our community. This year on Saturday, May 5th, we will celebrate Durham Refugee Day with live music, international food, games, and community education.
