Families Belong Together
\nA Rally and Press Conference Led by the North Carolina Chapter of Lawyer Moms of America and the Carolina Peace Center<\/p>\n

On Friday, June 29, together with community advocates and concerned citizens, lawyer moms around the country will gather at our Senators and Representatives\u2019 District Offices to deliver a nonpartisan open letter stating our opposition to the current federal policy of separating migrant children from their parents.<\/p>\n

To launch letter delivery in North Carolina, we will host a rally and press conference at 10 am, at Bicentennial Plaza in front of the Legislative building in downtown Raleigh. We will read our open letter, both in English and Spanish, and hear from other community leaders who are passionate about ending this horrifying and inhumane policy.<\/p>\n

Bring your families, and join us as we come together to launch delivery of our open letter, and support a weekend of advocacy.<\/p>\n
