On Saturday, October 7, in Cary, North Carolina, South Asian Americans Leading Together (SAALT), in collaboration with state and local officials, law enforcement, and community based organizations, will host a town hall to address the rising tide of hate violence aimed at South Asian American communities.<\/p>\n

Since the election, SAALT has documented over 168 incidents of hate violence against our South Asian, Muslim, Sikh, Hindu, Arab, and Middle Eastern American, already surpassing totals from the year leading up to the 2016 election. According to the Southern Poverty Law Center, anti-Muslim hate groups grew by 197% in 2016, and, according to the FBI, anti-Muslim hate crimes increased by 67% in 2015.<\/p>\n

In the face\u00a0of these staggering figures, we invite local community members to join us for a candid conversation on civil rights, hate violence, and the way forward to a more just and inclusive society for all Americans.<\/span><\/p>\n
