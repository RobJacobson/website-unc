The Center for New North Carolinians invites you and your youth groups to stand proudly for “presence.” We identify with our refugee\/immigrant youth and families that they prevail – seeking sufficiency, literacy, and respect.<\/p>\n

Over half of the world’s refugees are children. They may have witnessed or experienced violent acts and, in exile, are at risk of abuse, neglect, violence, exploration, trafficking or military recruitment. We stand with them for resettlement, aiding in their safe transition, and a successful integration.<\/p>\n
