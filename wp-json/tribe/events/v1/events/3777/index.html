Muslim Women For is proud to partner with the\u00a0American Friends Service Committee of the Carolinas<\/a>, a Quaker organization that has an extensive history of promoting lasting peace with justice, as a practical expression of faith in action. It is the mission of ‘Muslim Women For’ to provide resources and tangible strategies to promote leadership.<\/p>\n

This training is designed for a Muslim-only audience to unpack Islamophobia in-depth and strategize around meaningful ways for our communities to resist anti-Muslimism right now. Networking and base-building opportunities will be made available at the end of the program. This training is especially important for young activists, Muslim leaders, people of color and women.<\/p>\n

There are a very limited number of seats and only registered participants may attend.<\/p>\n

We would love to accommodate different learning styles and differently abled folks. Please let us know on the registration form how we can ensure you are comfortable in the space.<\/p>\n
