Carolina Peace Center welcomes you to a Panel on Muslim Travel Ban and anti-immigrant policies including DACA recipients. Community faith leaders, legal counsels and political leaders will be among the panelists. The discussion will be centered on draconian policy that is steeped in Islamophobia, racism and xenophobia. Further, it will also include dialogue about the anti-immigration policy that has resulted in the deportation of people who have lived in this country for decades. Thus, sending hard-working immigrants back to their countries where they face a very precarious future.<\/p>\n

\nList of panelists will be updated on this event page in coming days.<\/p>\n
