On April 12th – 20th 2018, Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) attacked our community in Western North Carolina resulting in 20+ family separations and kidnapping of our community members – mostly in Henderson County.<\/p>\n

Our Legal Enforcement has consistently proven to be on the side of injustice by collaborating with ICE and re-signing 287g for years. All of this with the premise and language Trump\u2019s administration uses to criminalize black and brown immigrants.<\/p>\n

Mijente is touring the country to unapologetically say CHINGA LA MIGRA!<\/p>\n

On June 14th, become. a resistor with us: we will use art and story telling to speak, heal and integrate the effects of the recent ICE raids, and strategy to further show that our community is united, that we are resisting ICE and will not take it anymore!<\/p>\n
