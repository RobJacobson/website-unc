Atention immigrant youth, ifamilies and allies:<\/p>\n

Local NC youth and adults with DACA are calling you to join the national group United We Dream in a march on Washington DC to demand a clean DREAM Act.<\/p>\n

We will depart Greensboro in caravan to Washington DC to demand our elected representatives to support this measure and then in the afternoon we will join the march.<\/p>\n

WE WILL HAVE TRANSPORTATION SUPPORT. MORE DETAILS LATER.
\n**Your help and participation is important; however, priority for transportation will be given to people with DACA. We need help from supporters with valid Drivers Licenses to drive, make and carry signs, take pictures, make calls to elected representatives and other support tasks that may come up.<\/p>\n

\nJovenes y adultos de NC con DACA les invitamos a unirnos al llamado del grupo nacional United We Dream en una marcha en Washington DC para exigir que el Congreso apruebe un DREAM Act limpio.<\/p>\n

Partiremos desde Greensboro en caravana con destino a Washington DC para exigirle a los legisladores que apoyen la medida y nos uniremos a la marcha por la tarde.<\/p>\n
