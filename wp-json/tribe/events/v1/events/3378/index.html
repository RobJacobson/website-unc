Appalachian State, NCSU, and Elon are featuring talks from renowned feminist and activist, Neesa Medina. \u00a0Neesa will present a feminist perspective of current events, and the impact of US policy in Honduras. See below for the schedule of events.<\/p>\n

Since the 2009 military coup in Honduras, murders and attacks against Honduran human rights defenders have reached alarming levels. The U.S. continues to send tens of millions of dollars in aid to the Honduran police and military who have been involved in human rights abuses. In addition, the U.S. backs neoliberal projects that lead to displacement and migration. Come and join us at one of our events to learn more!<\/p>\n

  • Friday October 20th: Elon, NC: Elon University 12:15 pm at Holt Chapel on South Campus<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
