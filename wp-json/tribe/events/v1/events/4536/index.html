Following the defeat of several anti-immigrant sheriff’s across NC our own Terry Johnson has demonstrated interest in entering into a partnership with ICE to interview anyone booked into the jail, for any reason, and not born into the US, for possible deportation. This agreement would also allow ICE to rent space in the jail – turning Alamance County Detention Center into a virtual ICE facility.<\/p>\n

Join neighbors, local business owners, and faith leaders as we come together for a press conference and rally before packing the county commissioners meeting to make it clear that residents in Alamance don’t want ICE in our county.<\/p>\n

Meet at 6 p.m. outside the Graham Historic Courthouse. Spread the word!
