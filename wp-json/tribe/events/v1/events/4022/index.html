Join community leaders and concerned residents of Alamance County in saying No to the return of 287 (g). We will be hearing from speakers and deliver a petition to our county commissioners demanding they do everything in their power to protect our families, friends, and neighbors. Meet at 7 a.m. for a rally and press conference and stay for the county commissioners meeting at 9 a.m. if you can.<\/p>\n

In years past it devastated our community by tearing families part, creating distrust with local law-enforcement, and cost tax payers over $800,000 in court fines due the Sheriff’s discriminatory practices. We simply don’t need 287 (g) in Alamance County!
