\nJoin us for a popular education workshop and discussion with Alicia Sevila Hidalgo from the Martin Luther King Center in Havana, Cuba about the history and effects of the US Blockade against Cuba and other US interventions across Latin America.<\/p>\n

Alicia lives and works in Matanzas, Cuba as a pastor, ecumenical leader, popular educator focusing and gender and liberation theology, and a prison educator.<\/p>\n
