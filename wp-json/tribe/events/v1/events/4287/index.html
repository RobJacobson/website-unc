El Futuro\u2019s La Mesita Learning Cohort Pilot #2
\nDando la Bienvenida – Latino Immigrant-Welcoming Practices:
\nCultivating Confianza by Being Engaging, Effective, and Culturally Responsive<\/p>\n

The La Mesita team will select 20 participants for our next Learning Cohort Pilot that will run from August – December 2018. There are no requirements for eligibility beyond completing and submitting the application and the La Mesita Learning Cohort Pilot #2 Participant Expectations & Agreement by\u00a0August 3, 2018<\/span><\/span>\u00a0. See Agreement for all dates and details. Selected participants will be notified by\u00a0August 14, 2018<\/span><\/span>.<\/p>\n

Questions? Contact Megan at\u00a0mrobertson@elfuturo-nc.org<\/a><\/p>\n

There is no cost to participants and a completion award of $500 is offered to those who complete all requirements.<\/p>\n

Priority will be given to applicants who:
\ndemonstrate commitment to full participation in all cohort activities
\ndemonstrate commitment to setting and reaching personal goals related to becoming more Latino immigrant-welcoming
\nwork in organizations\/practices that directly serve Latino immigrants in North Carolina
\nprovide mental health or substance use disorder treatment to Latino immigrants in North Carolina
\nserve as supervisor or administrator in organization\/practice that provides behavioral health services to Latino immigrants in North Carolina<\/p>\n

We look forward to receiving dozens of applications from professionals across the state who are aware of the needs and challenges that human service systems face in being responsive to the unique needs of the Latino immigrants who came to our state to build a better life. El Futuro is committed to increasing access to quality care for Latino immigrants by reducing provider isolation and burnout. We welcome this opportunity to learn from, and with, others who want to expand their capacity for cultural competence and who embrace the best practices that are needed to move North Carolina forward
