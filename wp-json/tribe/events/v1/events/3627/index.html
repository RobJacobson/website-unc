Movement to End Racism and Islamophobia invites everyone to join this workshop to brainstorm, compose, and submit Letters to the Editor across North Carolina newspapers. Please also bring your ideas on how to use digital media to challenge Islamophobia, including videos, spoken word, etc.<\/p>\n

Our goal is to change the media narrative around anti-Muslim bigotry! We will encourage folks to compose letters putting forward a narrative that rejects anti-Muslim bigotry alongside anti-Semitism, racism, anti-queer and anti-trans hatred, and US wars and occupations.<\/p>\n

We will met at The Lighthouse Project (202 N. Tarboro St. Raleigh, NC 27610). You can park onthe street, or in the parking lot across the street at the Tarboro Community Center.<\/p>\n

**Please bring a laptop or tablet if you have one! All are welcome, and no prior experience writing letters to the editor or creating is required.**<\/p>\n
