Artist Studio Project in collaboration with the COR Museum and M.T. Pope House Museum invite you to participate in a very special D\u00eda De Los Muertos Cultural Event. As part of the 3rd Annual El Quixote Festival honoring Sor Juana Ines de La Cruz, three special female artists will be creating an altar honoring the memory of the women who impacted us, who we love and we miss dearly and have passed on. We encourage the community to participate by submitting the name of a special person that has inspired them or they would like to honor. We will include their name in the altar.
