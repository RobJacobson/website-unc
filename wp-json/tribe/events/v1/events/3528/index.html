Every year Mecklenburg County holds their anual ” Steering Committee Meeting” to discuss the impact of the program in our community. The 287(g) program is a partnership between Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) and a state or local law enforcement entity.<\/p>\n

More than 10,000 people have been deported in Mecklenburg County because of this program, most for minor crimes.<\/p>\n

Mecklenburg County Sheriff Jim Pendergraph started the program in 2006, current Sheriff Irwin Carmichael has refused to end the program.<\/p>\n

287g has been a detramental program to the Charlotte Mecklenburg area since 2006 and continues to become even more dangerous in the era of Trump.<\/p>\n
