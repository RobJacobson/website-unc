Join us Monday evening at 6:30 to resist Islamophobia and Racism.<\/p>\n

As Muslim students and allies, we refuse to allow others to define us with tired, racist binaries. We are tired of being told that we have to \u201cchoose\u201d between being \u201cinsurgents\u201d and \u201cpatriots.\u201d We are tired of people claiming to represent us, while actively promoting violence against countless of Muslims across the globe. We are tired of people erasing our multiplicity, our struggles, and our lived realities. Join Duke MSA, Duke Muslim Students, and allies in a counter teach-in against Islamophobia and racism, and maybe we can have an honest conversation about our powerful and beautiful American Muslim Identities.<\/span><\/p>\n

Our teach-in comes in response to an event hosted by the Alexander Hamilton Society\u2019s event on \u201cThe American Muslim Identity,\u201d featuring Zuhdi Jasser, a man who claims to represent Muslims while painting us broadly as terrorists, supporting the Muslim ban, promoting surveillance of American Muslim communities, and generally advocating against Muslims and the Muslim community. The event was originally titled \u201cThe American Muslim Identity: Patriot or Insurgent?\u201d as if those are the only two camps into which Muslims in the United States can fall. The Alexander Hamilton Society\u2019s choice to rehash and weaponize \u201cwith us or against us\u201d rhetoric against Muslims is disappointing and disrespectful both of Duke\u2019s Muslim students
