Join us this WEDNESDAY MAY 16 to hear from the families of the detainees and the latest updates about the cases and what ways community can help reunite the families!!<\/p>\n

On April 14th, Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) conducted raids in Western North Carolina where over 26 people were detained. Over the last 3 weeks, two people affected by the raids have already been deported and others await deportation with fear of returning to a country they have not seen in years. They are still waiting for a decision on their cases as early as this week. Join us this Wednesday to hear from their family members and to hear an update and what community members can do to take action to help reunite these families.<\/p>\n

The ICE raids caused psychological and emotional turmoil to the families of the men that were detained, but to the WNC community as a whole. We believe that these men should be reunited with their families and their communities. Because no human being should undergo the treatment and conditions that people experience being detained in Stewart Detention Center.<\/p>\n
